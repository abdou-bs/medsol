about us


Bessaa and Cheurfa

The project to create a medical website by abderraouf and Hanna, two students in L2 Computer Science at the University of Algiers 1 for the DAW module, is a concrete example of the application of theoretical knowledge in web development.. Their passion and determination were key drivers in bringing this ambitious project to fruition. They hope that their medical website will be useful to users by providing valuable information and contributing to improved health and well-being. This project also allows them to gain hands-on experience and strengthen their programming skills, which will benefit their future careers.
